Support

Sources that you can support to help prevent the spread of microplastics!

The Ocean Cleanup

This nonprofit organization develops advanced technologies to remove plastic pollution from the oceans. They target larger plastic debris to prevent it from breaking down into microplastics and also work on intercepting plastic in rivers before it reaches the ocean.

[theoceancleanup.com]


Plastic Pollution Coalition

A global alliance that seeks to eliminate single-use plastics and reduce plastic pollution. They advocate for policy changes, educate the public, and support initiatives that address the impacts of plastics, including microplastics, on health and the environment.

[plasticpollutioncoalition.org]



Surfrider Foundation

Originally focused on protecting coastlines, this organization now has a broader mission, including the reduction of plastic pollution. Through campaigns, cleanups, and policy advocacy, they work to reduce plastic waste and microplastics in oceans and waterways.

[surfrider.org]



Break Free From Plastic

A global movement of organizations and individuals pushing for systemic changes to stop plastic pollution for good. This coalition works on policy advocacy, corporate accountability, and grassroots action to address plastic pollution, including microplastics.

[breakfreefromplastic.org]!
